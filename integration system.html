Integration Instructions

1. Click the 'Connect' button next to the system you wish to integrate.

2. You will be redirected to the provider's website to authorize the connection.

3. Enter your system credentials and grant CampusConnect access to your data.

4. Once connected, data synchronization will begin automatically. You can manage or disconnect integrations at any time from this screen.
